📚 About ShelfBuddy

ShelfBuddy is your personal library companion! Whether you're managing your growing book collection, tracking what you're reading, setting goals, or loaning books to friends — ShelfBuddy makes it easy and fun.

✨ Features:

  • 📘 Catalog and manage your entire personal library
  • 📈 Set and track personalized reading goals
  • 📝 Maintain a wishlist of books to read next
  • 📕 Track loaned and borrowed books with due dates
  • 🔎 Search for new books using integrated tools
  • 🎯 Get progress reports and suggestions if falling behind

💡 Why ShelfBuddy?

Reading is more than just flipping pages — it's about setting goals, celebrating achievements, and enjoying every story. ShelfBuddy helps you stay organized and inspired, turning every book into a milestone on your personal journey. 📖✨
